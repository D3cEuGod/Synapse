Our Philosophy

Wellbeing is multi-dimensional. That's why Synapse doesn't just focus on a single metric. We blend physical tracking, mental health check-ins, and human connection into one cohesive programme.

  • Proactive rather than reactive mental health support.

  • Connecting colleagues through secure, trained peer support.

  • Rewarding positive habits with a built-in gamification system.

Privacy First

Your personal health data is strictly confidential. HR and management only see anonymised, aggregated trends to help improve workplace culture—never individual profiles or sensitive information.

A Holistic Approach to Wellbeing

Everything your team needs to thrive, built on a secure, gamified foundation.

Mental Health Tracking

Daily mood logging and clinical self-assessments help identify risks early. Get automated recommendations for support when you need it most.

Physical Activity & Diet

Log your steps, monitor activity intensity, and track meals. Visualise your daily net calories and hit your fitness targets seamlessly.
